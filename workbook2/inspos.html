INSPIRATIONS

Pangpangpang’s playful chaotic collage style has inspired me to create a more energetic and freespirited style of workbook. Their designs feel spontanious and confident which I love.

Ji Park’s website feels simple and elegant while still being highly interactive which I love. I was inspired by how the motion and layout feel smooth and thoughtful, which influenced how I structured and paced my own workbook pages.

Her portfolio feels very raw and nostalgic yet it still feels clean and modern at the same time.

This catalogue has inspired some of my layouts. I like the combination of simple serious sans serif fonts with playful design elements and shapes.
