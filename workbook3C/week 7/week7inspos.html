Treachery of Sanctuary - An interactive art instillaton that uses birds as a metaphor for transformation and loss.This was one of the first pieces I came across when looking into physical computing and thought it looked cool. It was a nice introduction into physical interactive art.

E-Traces - A device that captures a dancer's movements using wearable sensors and translates them into data. This made me think about how everyday movements could be recorded and interpreted creatively. It has inspired me to consider how physical computing can be used in an artistic and expressive way rather than just functionally.

Cursor Effects - A list of cursor effects that are nostalgic and fun. This inspired me to use cursors as part of the design (the multiplying flies on the experiments and ideas sectioin). This also sparked one of the many brainstorms for the final project = a clingy mouse.
